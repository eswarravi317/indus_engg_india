About Us

The Company wholly owned by INDUSTRIAL ENGINEERS (INDIA), RAJKUMAR was established in 1985 (Enriched experience of more than three decades) with blessings of the GOD, Sincerity & Hard works.

INDUSTRIAL ENGINEERS (INDIA) - (Professionally oriented customer driven team of dedicated technocrats) Works was started at Chennai- Chromepet Unit, as a small workshop to cater to Repair needs of small Induction motors up to 50 H.P. Through Slow & constant development with Really, Genuinity & hard-work, Today it is one of the leading service industry in India.

Repairs / Servicing & refurbishment of Rotating Machines(Rotors and Armatures) up-to 2 MW with all in-house facilities. Presently, with a team of around 12 experienced technicians and Engineering personnel, and 15-Semi- Skilled Company is able to cater to the repairs / rehabilitation of all types of Rotating Electrical Machines both at their Works. as well as at site.

The Company is also in to Export of High Voltage (11 KV) Stator Coils for Induction Motors, Armature Coils, Commutator For DC Motors.

The Company has Semi fledged Computerized Design capability to take up Design modifications of existing machines.

We undertake Repairs/Re-windings of all types of AC/DC with HT/LT Induction & traction motors, Generators, Alternators, LT & HT- Transformers(33KV/11KV), AC/DC SERVO motors, Spindle Motors, Stepped Motors.

INDUSTRIAL ENGINEERS (INDIA) Service Covered with All Automobile, Food & Beverages, Glass, Steel Rolling & Forging Plant, Spinning Mills & Textile Industries, Tyre & Polyurethane Plants, Chemical & Pharmaceuticals Companies.

We also manufacture and supply special Transformers, LT motors, Special Solenoid Coils, Brake Coils for Hoists & Other relevant Coils.
